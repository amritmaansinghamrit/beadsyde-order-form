What is a Permanent Bracelet?

A permanent bracelet is a delicate chain that is welded closed around your wrist - no clasp, no fuss, just a beautiful piece of jewelry that stays with you 24/7. It's the ultimate symbol of commitment, friendship, or self-love.

What to expect during your appointment:

  • Choose your chain style (silver, gold, or rose gold)
  • We'll measure and fit the bracelet perfectly to your wrist
  • Using a safe, painless welding process, we'll close the chain
  • Walk out with your forever bracelet! The entire process takes about 10-15 minutes
